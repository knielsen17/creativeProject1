Types of Distributions

Continuous Distributions

  • Normal Distribution

  • A standard bell curve with limits of negavtive infinity to positive infinity

  • Gamma Distribution

  • A very common distribution with limits of zero to positive infinity

  • Beta Distribution

  • Completely changable distribution with limits of zero to one

  • Continuous Uniform Distribution

  • This distribution makes the probability of all possible events equal

Discrete Distributions

  • Bernoulli Distribution

  • Takes values of one of zero

  • Binomial Distribution

  • This is a probability of a number of successes with indepenent trials with the same probability

  • Poisson Distribution

  • This is n number of bernoulli trials
