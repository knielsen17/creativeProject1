Poisson Distribution

The poisson distribution is the most commonly used distribution of the discrete distributions because the calculations are easier than others. The distribution is also a counting distribution that goes from 0 to infinity. This increases its usefulness because many counting things have no upper limit. The mean of the distribution is also the only parameter. This makes estimating simple things in life very easy. For example, I could approximate that on average I ask a question in class per week is 3. I could calculate the probability that I ask 7 or more questions in one week very easily. And if someone has a different mean, I only have to change that and calculate again. The most unique trait though is the mean and variance are equal.
