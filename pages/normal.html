Normal Distribution

The normal distribution is the easily the most famous of all the distributions. It is the first distribution that everyone learns about. The distribution has the limits of negative infinity to infinity. There really is no closed form cdf, so it makes it hard to do a lot of the probability calculations. That is why every intro stats class teaches you how to use those tables. This distribution does very well with convolutions. You can sum the distribution, and the distribution is still a normal distribution. Also the only parameter are the mean and the standard deviation. This distribution is also symmetrically and can't be changed from being symmetrical. This makes this distribution useful to model only symmetrical data.
