Exponential Distribution

The exponential distribution is a specific case of the gamma distribution with a shape parameter of 1. The exponential distribution is a useful computationally easy gamma distribution. This distribution is often used in kernel math when attempting to simplify more complicated distributions. The exponential distribution is also mixed with other distributions very often. The variance is always smaller than the mean. This is also true of the gamma distribution. This makes these two unique. If the data has a variance that is clearly less than the mean, then one of the best distributions to model the data is probably a gamma or exponential distribution.
