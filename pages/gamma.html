Gamma Distribution

The gamma distribution is one of the most used continuous distributions ever. The shape parameter is k. The scale parameter is &Theta. These parameters allow you to modify the distribution to model many different things. The variance will always be greater than mean. The mean and variance are easy to calculate too. With the modifiable shape and scale, the gamma distribution is almost completely customizable.
